# Script Reference Guide

## Example 1: Lemon Sparkling Water Ad (30s Douyin Vertical)

**Concept**: A fresh, artistic summer beverage ad using everyday scenes to convey the product's refreshing coolness.

```yaml
meta:
  duration_seconds: 30
  aspect_ratio: "9:16"
  platform: "douyin"
  style: "cinematic"

concept:
  one_liner: "Showcase the freshness and thirst-quenching quality of lemon sparkling water through a lazy summer afternoon"
  mood: "Lazy, refreshing, soothing"
  color_palette: ["#FFF8DC", "#90EE90", "#87CEEB"]

scenes:
  - id: "S01"
    title: "Sunlight Opening"
    duration_seconds: 4
    description: "Sunlight filters through white linen curtains onto a natural wood table surface. On the table lies an open book and a pair of sunglasses. Warm tones, quiet scene."
    camera: "close-up, static, shallow depth of field"

  - id: "S02"
    title: "Retrieving the Product"
    duration_seconds: 5
    description: "A hand opens a retro white refrigerator and takes out a bottle of lemon sparkling water covered in condensation droplets. The bottle is transparent with pale yellow liquid inside."
    camera: "medium shot, eye-level"

  - id: "S03"
    title: "Bottle Opening Close-up"
    duration_seconds: 4
    description: "Close-up of a hand twisting open the bottle cap. Bubbles surge out, mist billows, background blurred."
    camera: "extreme close-up, slow motion"

  - id: "S04"
    title: "Pouring into Glass"
    duration_seconds: 5
    description: "Sparkling water is poured into a glass filled with ice cubes and lemon slices. Bubbles churn in the glass as the lemon slice spins."
    camera: "close-up, 45-degree angle, slow motion"

  - id: "S05"
    title: "Enjoyment Moment"
    duration_seconds: 5
    description: "On a balcony, a small table beside a rattan chair holds the glass of sparkling water. Green plants and blue sky in the distance."
    camera: "medium wide, dolly back slowly"

  - id: "S06"
    title: "Product Display"
    duration_seconds: 4
    description: "Product bottle centered, gradient solid color background, brand logo appears."
    camera: "static, studio lighting"

  - id: "S07"
    title: "Closing CTA"
    duration_seconds: 3
    description: "Brand slogan text appears with refreshing visual effects."
    camera: "static"
```

## Example 2: Food Exploration (45s Bilibili Horizontal)

**Concept**: A first-person perspective visit to a hidden old Beijing hotpot restaurant tucked away in a hutong alley.

Key points:
- 16:9 horizontal format, better for showcasing the environment
- First 3 seconds use a suspense hook: "Inside Beijing's Second Ring Road, there's a hotpot restaurant that's only open 4 hours a day"
- Sound design is important: the sound of meat being dipped, meat being sliced, the owner's calls
- Faster visual pacing, 2-4 seconds per shot

## Style Reference

### Fresh & Artistic
- Soft natural light
- Shallow depth of field
- Warm tones (Japanese color palette)
- Slow-paced editing
- Ambient sounds as primary audio

### Dynamic & Energetic
- High contrast
- Fast editing
- Strong rhythmic soundtrack
- Moving camera
- Vivid colors

### Cinematic
- 2.35:1 letterbox
- Cool-warm tone contrast
- Long takes
- Shallow depth of field
- Film grain

### Minimalist Modern
- Solid color backgrounds
- Geometric composition
- Sans-serif typography
- Product centered
- Generous white space
